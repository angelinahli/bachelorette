The twelfth season of The Bachelorette, an American reality competition television series premiered on ABC on May 23, 2016,[1] and concluded on August 1, 2016. The show featured Joelle "JoJo" Fletcher, a real estate developer from Dallas, Texas and former runner-up on the 20th season of The Bachelor, in the title role where she is courted by 26 men.[2] Former pro quarterback Jordan Rodgers won the competition.

Filming and development[edit]

Casting and contestants[edit]

Casting began during season eleven of The Bachelorette. Days before the official announcement, Fletcher's fellow The Bachelor season 20 contestant and second runner-up Caila Quinn was originally selected to be in the lead role,[3] but she was dropped at the last minute for Fletcher by the producers in response to the popularity of Fletcher.[4]

Filming commenced on March 16, 2016 shortly after the twentieth season of The Bachelor. Destinations including Pittsburgh in Pennsylvania, Uruguay, Thailand, and Argentina with appearances from Dan + Shay, All-4-One, Max Kellerman and Marcellus Wiley.[5][6][7]

The cast includes former American football quarterback Jordan Rodgers, the younger brother of Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers.[8]

Future appearances[edit]

Bachelor in Paradise[edit]

Season 3

Christian Bishop, Chad Johnson, Daniel Maguire, Evan Bass, Grant Kemp, Vinny Venteria, and Wells Adams were all chosen for the third season of Bachelor in Paradise. Johnson was removed from the show by Chris Harrison in episode 1 for aggressive behaviour towards everyone. Neither Bishop, Venteria, Maguire, nor Adams found love. Bass and Kemp got engaged to Bachelor Nation alums, Carly Waddell and Lace Morris, respectively. Kemp and Morris broke up shortly after the conclusion of the season. Bass and Waddell got married on the fourth season of Bachelor in Paradise.

Season 4

Venteria and Maguire competed in the fourth season of Bachelor in Paradise. They were joined by first-time returnees Nick Benvenutti, Alex Woytkiw, Derek Peth, and Robby Hayes. Adams also returned in this season as the hotel bartender replacing Jorge Moreno. Woytkiw, Benvenutti, and Venteria were all eliminated in week one. Hayes quit in week four. Maguire left in a relationship with Lacey Mark, but it was revealed on the reunion special that they had split shortly afterwards. Peth left in a relationship with Taylor Nolan, and the two were engaged on the reunion special.

Australia season 1

Kemp and Maguire were seen on promos for the Australian version of Bachelor in Paradise.[38]

Celebrity Big Brother UK[edit]

Johnson competed on Celebrity Big Brother.[39] After 25 days in the house, he placed 5th.

The Bachelor Winter Games[edit]

Luke Pell appeared in The Bachelor Winter Games to compete as Team USA. He was eliminated in week four.

Ex On The Beach[edit]

Chase McNary will appear in MTV reality series Ex on the Beach.
